Privacy Option Details

When submitting your RSVP, you can choose to keep your response private. Private RSVPs remain invisible in public event listings but are fully visible to the event host. This allows you to maintain discretion while still providing the host with complete information.

  • • Public lists will not show your name or details
  • • Event host can still see your full RSVP information
  • • You can change privacy setting after submitting
  • • Applies to all RSVP statuses (Yes, No, Maybe)
