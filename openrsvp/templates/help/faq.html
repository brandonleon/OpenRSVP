Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about OpenRSVP.

OpenRSVP is a privacy-focused event management platform that allows you to create, share, and manage events without requiring user accounts or storing personal information.

Magic links are unique, one-time access tokens that allow you to edit your RSVP or manage an event without creating an account. They are generated when you first create or RSVP to an event and can be saved locally in your browser.
